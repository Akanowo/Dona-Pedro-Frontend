About us

Dona Pedro Agencies is a family-owned business that was registered in 31 August 2012 by Ukandu Pedro Onyemeachi. We have delivered world-standard services in different areas over the years and have succeeded across boards. From deep cleaning, to lifestyle entertainment and now car rentals. Our delivery of excellent service meets and exceeds the expectations of esteemed clients with maximum client satisfaction guaranteed is what has sustained us over the years.
